Faith Jensen | Exoplanets & Stellar Activity

I earned my BA in Physics & Astronomy from the University of Montana, where my love for exoplanet science began. My current research focuses on performing injection and recovery simulations with quasi-periodic Gaussian Process models to quantify how stellar activity biases low-mass planet parameter estimates.
