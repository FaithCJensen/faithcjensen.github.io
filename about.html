About Me

I was born and raised in Montana, and I love everything about the night sky. I am specifically passionate about exoplanets and stellar activity, and contributing to the detection of low-mass planets! My goal is to advance exoplanet research, while sharing the wonder of the universe, and collaborating with curious minds along the way. :)

In addition to research, I enjoy playing volleyball, reading, and capturing the night sky with my Seestar S30 telescope.
