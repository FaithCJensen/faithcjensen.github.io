About Me

I am a researcher and science communicator passionate about astronomy, exoplanets, and stellar activity. My work focuses on understanding complex astrophysical phenomena while making them accessible to the public.

I combine observational data, statistical modeling, and visualization to analyze radial velocity signals, detect exoplanets, and explore the dynamics of distant worlds.

In addition to research, I enjoy sharing knowledge through writing, presentations, and open science projects, bridging the gap between data and understanding for diverse audiences.
