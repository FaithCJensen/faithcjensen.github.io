Research Highlights

My research focuses on how variability in stars affects the detection of planets via radial velocity measurements. Stellar active regions can mimic or obscure planetary signals, making it challenging to identify Earth-like exoplanets around Sun-like stars. My work aims to disentangle these effects to improve detection reliability.
